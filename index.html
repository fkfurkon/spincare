How SpinCare Works

SpinCare is a real medical device that uses electrospinning to create nanofiber wound dressings. A high-voltage electric field stretches a polymer solution into ultra-fine fibers (50-500nm) that land on the wound, forming a breathable protective layer.

In This Simulation

Move your mouse to aim the device. Hold click to spray nanofibers onto the wound. Cover 100% to complete the dressing!

Power slider: Higher power = wider spray but less precision.
Polymer: Changes the fiber color and properties.
